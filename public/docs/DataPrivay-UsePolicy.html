StudEx Data Privacy & Ethical Use Policy

Purpose Scope Legal & Regulatory Compliance Lawful Basis Data Collection User Rights Data Security Ethical Use Breach Response Minors Data Retention Policy Review

Purpose

This policy outlines how STUDEX collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data. It also defines the ethical standards guiding user interactions and data handling across the platform.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • All STUDEX platform users (buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants)
  • STUDEX employees, contractors, and third‑party service providers
  • All data processing, storage, and access via web, mobile, and integrated systems

Legal & Regulatory Compliance

STUDEX complies with applicable laws, including:

  • GDPR where EU/EEA users are involved
  • NDPR for Nigerian data subjects
  • Any other relevant jurisdictional data protection laws

Cross‑border data transfers will only occur using accepted safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

Lawful Basis for Data Processing

STUDEX processes personal data only under valid legal bases:

  • Explicit user consent
  • Contract performance
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate interest (without overriding user rights)

Data Collection & Minimization

STUDEX collects only necessary data, such as:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers
  • Property details and preferences
  • Identification or verification documents (only when required)

Sensitive data is avoided unless strictly necessary and legally justified.

User Consent & Transparency

  • A clear and accessible Privacy Notice
  • Explicit consent for sharing, marketing, and sensitive data use
  • Options to opt in/out of communications
  • No manipulative consent mechanisms

User Rights

STUDEX users can:

  • Access their data
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability
  • Object to profiling or automated decisions

A dedicated privacy contact channel will be provided.

Data Security & Protection

STUDEX implements appropriate safeguards:

  • Encryption (transit and storage)
  • Role‑based access control (RBAC)
  • Secure authentication (e.g., MFA)
  • Logging and monitoring
  • Regular vulnerability assessments

Data is retained only for its intended purpose.

Ethical Data Use

STUDEX commits to:

  • No unauthorized resale of user data
  • No hidden profiling or surveillance
  • No digital discrimination or exclusion (race, ethnicity & religion)
  • Transparent criteria in recommendation features
  • Avoiding fake listings, bots, and deceptive content

Third‑Party Service Providers

When STUDEX engages third parties:

  • Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) will be in place
  • Only necessary data will be shared
  • Vendors must follow equivalent data protection standards
  • Cross‑border transfers follow legal requirements

Data Breach Response

If a breach occurs, STUDEX will:

  • Activate its incident response plan (Preparation, Identification, Containment, Eradication, Recovery & Lessons Learned)
  • Contain and assess the incident immediately
  • Notify authorities within required timelines (e.g., 72 hours under GDPR & NDPR)
  • Inform affected users when necessary
  • Document causes and remedies

Protection of Minors and Vulnerable Users

  • Age restrictions or declarations will be applied where appropriate
  • Parental or guardian consent will be obtained where required
  • No targeted ads to minors

Governance & Accountability

  • Appoint a Data Protection Officer or privacy lead (if required)
  • Maintain records of processing activities
  • Conduct privacy training for staff
  • Perform DPIAs when data risks are high

Ethical Communication & Advertising

  • Communications are truthful and transparent
  • Free from bias or manipulation
  • Clear about sponsored or paid content
  • Consent‑based for marketing activities

Data Retention & Disposal

STUDEX will only retain data as long as needed for:

  • Service delivery
  • Legal or regulatory obligations
  • Contract fulfillment

Afterward, data will be securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with NDPR guidelines.

Policy Review & Updates

This policy will be reviewed annually or when:

  • Laws change
  • New technologies are implemented
  • New processing activities begin

Updates will be communicated to affected stakeholders.

Enforcement

Non‑compliance with this policy may result in:

  • Disciplinary action
  • Vendor contract termination
  • User suspension or removal

If you have questions about this policy, contact STUDEX's privacy team via the app or at privacy@studex.ng.
